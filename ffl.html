Our Projects

  • The Park Project
    We've committed to constructing new spaces for nature inside cities to bring a taste of the natural world into places where they are not usually seen.
  • 2F2P
    The Forest Fire Prevention Project aims to prevent the spread and start of wildfires which can devastate natural environments.
  • Habitat Protection
    We want to help preserve the natural habitats of wildlife across the world from logging and commercial natural destruction.
  • The Plant Fund
    The Plant Fund spends all raised money solely into the planting of trees everywhere around the world.
  • Volunteer Team
    Our team of incredible volunteers help Forests for Life in all of our projects, learn more about becoming a volunteer today.
  • Community Education
    Our mission is nothing without education. We hold workshops and sessions throughout communities to educate people on the need for change.
